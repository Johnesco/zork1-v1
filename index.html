Zork I — v1: The Port

ZIL to Inform 7 Translation

Play Play in IF Hub Source Walkthrough

The first complete, playable, winnable translation of Zork I from ZIL to Inform 7. 110 rooms, 138 objects, every puzzle faithfully reproduced. The goal: a 1:1 gameplay match with the original.

Claude Code performed the initial translation in a single pass — it compiled on the first try. But “compiles” does not mean “correct.” The real work began in comparing transcripts side by side, discovering what the translation missed. 377 missing text responses were restored from ZIL source in a 6-phase audit (#44).

What’s in v1

  • 110 rooms across five regions, all 19 treasures, 350-point scoring system with ranked titles
  • NPCs: roaming thief (5-turn patrol cycle, 75% steal rate, treasure redistribution), troll combat with unconscious/recovery mechanic, cyclops with multiple solutions
  • Full puzzle systems: dam and reservoir, exorcism ceremony, coal-to-diamond machine, inflatable boat and river, mirror rooms
  • 377 missing text responses restored from ZIL source in a 6-phase audit
  • Dead player ghost state — explore as spirit with dimly-lit rooms and prayer resurrection
  • Sword glow scans adjacent rooms and detects all three villains, matching ZIL logic

What’s NOT in v1

No bug fixes beyond what the modern engine naturally corrects. No sound. No visual effects. This is the raw translation — if it was in the ZIL, it’s in v1. The original bugs that survived the engine transition are still here. Improvement begins in v2.
